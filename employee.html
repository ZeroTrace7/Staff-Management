Background Power Usage Restrictions

Your device manufacturer is known to have aggressive power management that may stop this app from tracking attendance correctly.

For Xiaomi/Redmi devices:
1. Settings > Apps > Manage apps > Staff Management
2. Turn ON "Autostart"
3. Under "Battery saver", select "No restrictions"
4. Settings > Battery & performance > App battery saver
5. Find Staff Management and set to "No restrictions"
6. Lock the app in Recent Apps (swipe down on the app preview)

⚠️ Without proper settings, attendance tracking may not work reliably.
